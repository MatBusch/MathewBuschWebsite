The VR Museum

The VR Museum is an IOS VR application that utilises the portable Google Carboard setup to allow users to enter worlds through gazing into paintings and also observe highly rendered 3D scanned statues and relics that would normally be an expensive once in a lifetime trip to see in person.

The Circle Of life

The circle of life was a projection mapped visualisation that depicted the connectivness of everything from water through to cells through to the stars. Use the button below to watch the video where we discuss our process and also to view a recording of the piece.

The Big Red Run Documentary

The Big Red Run is a week long event that sees athletes from all over the globe run a series of Marathons and one Ultra marathon over consecutive days through the Australian Simpson Desert all to raise money for type 1 Diabeties. This 40 minute documentary I filmed and edited is the story of one athlete and his journey through the Aussie desert.

NSW Water Consumtion
