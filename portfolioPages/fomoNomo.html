FOMO NOMO

A solution to Social media driven fear of missing out in young people through wearable tech and Mindfulness - Wellbeing excercises.

A Project by
Mathew Busch, Colin Li and Jess Norris
November, 2017

Summary of the solution

A system that utilises wearable technologies to monitor FOMO (The Fear Of Missing Out) while using social media. The app provides access to the live data produced from the Belt (Breathing data) and Watch (Heart Rate data) that combine to display a users FOMO levels.

When the app is triggered by high levels of Fomo users are notified via the app and offered a range of mindfulness and awareness exercises to help reduce their Fomo and its possible long term effects.

The Brief

The original brief set by my University was to create a product to benifit the health industry.

After researching into current and future trends sourounding mental illnesses we came across a large variety of data and articles on the topic of Fomo. At that point we had decided to focus our concept and product prototype on.... Our youth.

From here we looked to research into what FOMO is and who is creating products for it already. We also delved into what other wearable tech was out in the world to help build inspiration for our design.
