FOMO NOMO

The FOMO NOMO is a combination of a smartwatch and belt that connects to an application that monitors your heart rate and breathing levels whenever connected to social media. If the system detects that you are experiencing Fear Of Missing Out (FOMO) the app will then offer you a choice of exercises to complete to help with your mindfulness and stress levels associated with social media use.
